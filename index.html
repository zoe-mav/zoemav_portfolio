About Me

Hi, I'm Zoe, a builder, researcher, and product thinker working at the intersection of engineering, artificial intelligence, and Web3.

Currently, I lead the Venture Capital Project at Crunch Foundation, where I've built an end-to-end pipeline that includes data ingestion, machine learning predictions, backtesting, A/B testing, and an interactive investment dashboard, all designed to make early-stage investment decisions more structured and scalable. Working with a heavily imbalanced dataset and noisy signals, my goal is to bring order and insight to one of the most unpredictable asset classes.

Before that, I conducted research at the Luxembourg Institute of Health, where I studied how vocal biomarkers evolved over the 14-day period of infection in COVID-19 patients. That project gave me the opportunity to code signal-processing models and design the UI for the COLIVE app.

Outside of work, you'll find me on the football pitch, carving down a mountain, listening to music, exploring 3D animation, or diving into cybersecurity; always learning, always building.
