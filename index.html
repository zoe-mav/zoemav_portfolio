About Me

Hi, I'm Zoe, an engineer, researcher, and product thinker passionate about leveraging technology to solve complex problems. I specialize in the intersection of engineering, artificial intelligence, and Web3, with a focus on building data-driven infrastructure that creates meaningful impact.

I'm Co-founder and CTO at Orion Finance, building institutional-grade portfolio management in DeFi. We deliver vault infrastructure for verifiable, privacy-preserving portfolio management, smart contract infrastructure that automates asset management and safeguards strategy confidentiality using Fully Homomorphic Encryption (FHE). My work bridges traditional institutional standards with DeFi innovation.

Before Orion, I led the Venture Capital Project at CrunchDAO, where I built an end-to-end pipeline for data-driven investment decisions: data ingestion, ML predictions, backtesting, and an interactive investment dashboard processing data for over 2M startups and 28M founders. Earlier, I conducted research at the Luxembourg Institute of Health on vocal biomarkers in COVID-19 patients and designed the UI for the COLIVE app.

Outside of work, you'll find me co-leading Highlanders in Clash of Clans, dropping into Verdansk, out on the football pitch, carving down a mountain, listening to music, or exploring 3D animation; always learning, always building.
